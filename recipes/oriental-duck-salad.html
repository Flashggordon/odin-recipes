Lam goi vit bap cai hanh tay (Oriental duck salad)

Discription

Duck salad is a dish that many people love because its so simple to make and doesn't take much time
it's easy to eat, it's extremely flavorful

  • 2 duck breast
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 a green mango
  • 1/4 cucumber
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 2 birds eye chili
  • 20g basil
  • 1 tbsp fish scauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 10g ginger
  • sprinkling of msg

Directions

  1. Boil duck breast with ginger
  2. Once cooked through drain and slice
  3. Peel green mango and onion
  4. Slice mango, onion and cucumber thinly
  5. Chop garlic, chili until puree
  6. Combine garlic chili with sugar, msg, and fish scauce and stir well
  7. Combine all ingredients together in a boal and serve
  8. Enjoy your meal
